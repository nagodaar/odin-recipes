INGREDIENTS

  • 1.5 lbs of raw chicken, cut doesn't matter as long as it shreds!
  • 2x 8oz blocks of cream cheese
  • A fuck ton of hot sauce (Frank's is tradition!)
  • Seasonings to taste, add some depth not just spice!
  1. Cut the chicken into larger chunks.
  2. Brown it all the way around the outside
  3. Dump in almost all the hot sauce, all the cream cheese, and a little butter
  4. Pressure cook that mf on high for about 45 minutes.
  5. I'm sure there was more, but really who cares? It's dip ya moron just make it.
  6. Be better than me, top it with chives and don't throw half of it our 3.5 weeks later.

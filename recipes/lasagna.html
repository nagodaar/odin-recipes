Lasagna Like Mom Used To Make

Have you ever had a craving not just for italian food, but something that fills the soul as well as the stomach? Use this recipe like a time machine back to simplier, tastier times.

All good food takes time, so don't commit to this recipe unless you're willing to go the distance. But remember, anything worth doing is worth doing well and this recipe is no different! When done correctly, this recipe will be the talk of the town! And, in classic italian fashion, no measurements are included. Season with your eyes and your heart!

Ingredients

  • 2 boxes of lasagna noodles
  • At least 1.5 jars worth of your favorite sauce (shoot for homemade!)
  • Dried Parsley
  • Dried Rosemary
  • Dried Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
